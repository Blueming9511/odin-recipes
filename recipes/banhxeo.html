Banh xeo's Recipes

Description

Bánh xèo (Vietnamese: [ɓǎjŋ̟ sɛ̂w], lit. 'sizzling pancake') is a crispy, stuffed rice pancake popular in Vietnam

ingredients:

  • - 2 cups water (or more)
  • - Salt and pepper to taste.
  • - A small amount of oil for frying the vegetables or meat - Vegetable: carrots, broccoli, cauliflower, spinach(optional), kale(optional)
  • - Banh xeo's powder

Step

  1. Prepare the batter
    • In a mixing bowl, combine 1 cup of rice flour, 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder (for color), 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 1 cup of coconut milk.
    • Mix well until smooth and let the batter rest for about 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the fillings:
    • Heat a pan over medium heat and add 1 tablespoon of oil.
    • Add 1/2 cup of diced onions and sauté until they become translucent.
    • Add 1 cup of thinly sliced pork belly or shrimp (or both) and cook until they are cooked through and slightly browned.
    • Remove the mixture from the pan and set it aside.
  3. Cook the banh xeo
  4. Add the filling
  5. Serve

~enjoy~
